PROJECT

SAFEHAVEN

SafeHaven is a women’s safety and support platform designed to provide a secure digital space for women in need. The platform offers real-time safety features, emergency assistance, and mental health resources, empowering women to navigate the world with confidence.

Rx ACCESS CARD

An Rx Access Card is a prescription discount card designed to help individuals save money on medications. These cards provide discounts on a wide range of prescription drugs at participating pharmacies. They are typically available for free and can be used by individuals who are uninsured, underinsured, or even those with insurance to get better pricing on medications.

FLIGHT PRICE FORECASTING DURING HOLIDAYS

Airfare prices fluctuate significantly, especially during peak holiday seasons, making it challenging for travelers to secure affordable tickets. This project aims to predict flight prices during holidays using IBM SPSS Modeler, a powerful data analytics and machine learning tool. By analyzing historical flight pricing data, trends, and influencing factors, the model provides insights that help travelers and travel agencies make informed booking decisions.
